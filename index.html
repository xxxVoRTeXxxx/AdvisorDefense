Why AdvisorDefense?

As fiduciaries, Registered Investment Advisers (RIAs) have an ongoing obligation to prioritize their clients' best interests, including safeguarding client data and protecting them from cyber threats. The SEC's latest regulations on cybersecurity risk management emphasize this responsibility, with upcoming rules specifically for investment advisors projected to be ratified in 2024.

AdvisorDefense provides meticulously crafted services that align with these key requirements, ensuring compliance while minimizing disruption for our clients.
