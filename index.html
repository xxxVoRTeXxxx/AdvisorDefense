SEC Regulation S-P Compliance

On May 15, 2024, the SEC adopted amendments to Regulation S-P, requiring RIAs to enhance policies for safeguarding client information. AdvisorDefense helps you comply with these mandates.

Incident Response Program

Our solutions help you detect, respond to, and recover from unauthorized access to client data, with policies for assessment, containment, and client notification within 30 days.

Service Provider Oversight

We ensure your service providers have robust controls, with due diligence and monitoring to protect client information, including notifications within 72 hours of a breach.

Service Comparison

AdvisorDefense offers tailored cybersecurity and compliance solutions at different levels to meet your needs.

Essentials

  • Tailored for smaller RIAs with up to 10 supervised persons. This tier offers foundational cybersecurity support with a set number of hours.

Advantage

  • Designed for mid-sized RIAs with 10 to 25 supervised persons. This tier builds on Essentials, providing additional hours of support to handle more complex requirements.

Advantage Plus

  • Geared toward larger RIAs with over 25 supervised persons. This tier includes all benefits of Advantage with significantly more hours, enabling comprehensive protection for larger, more complex operations.

Vendor Risk Management Only

  • Focused solution for overseeing service providers, including due diligence and access to our library of over 200 reports.
